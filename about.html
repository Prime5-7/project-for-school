History of Kaido,'Emperor of the sea':one piece
Before anyone knew the name Kaido, an old legend claimed there was a mountain in the New World where the wind itself refused to blow. Curious, Kaido climbed it alone and found an enormous bronze bell hanging from a dead tree. An inscription read, "Strike this bell only if you have no fear." Kaido hit the bell with a single punch. Instead of making a sound, the mountain shook, and hundreds of blue dragons made entirely of clouds rose into the sky before vanishing. The old pirates who witnessed the event believed the mountain had accepted him as its guardian. Every ten years, Kaido secretly returned to the mountain to ring the silent bell. Each visit reminded him that not every battle was won with fists. Some mysteries could only be faced with patience. After the mountain eventually disappeared beneath the sea during a violent storm, no one could prove the legend had ever been true. The only clue left behind was the silent bronze bell, drifting across the ocean without ever making a sound.
